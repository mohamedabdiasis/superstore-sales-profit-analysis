# 📊 Superstore Sales & Profitability Dashboard

An interactive, insight-driven Power BI dashboard built to analyze the Global Superstore dataset. This project transforms raw sales data into a compelling visual story, highlighting key performance indicators, regional trends, and critical areas for business improvement.

## 📌 About the Project
The goal of this dashboard is to move beyond basic visuals and focus on **actionable business insights**. It answers critical questions such as: 
- Which products and regions are driving actual profit (not just revenue)?
- How do shipping modes and discounts impact the bottom line?
- Where are the hidden inefficiencies in the business?

## 📊 Key Business Insights
Based on the data analysis, the dashboard reveals several critical takeaways:
1. **Overall Health**: The business generates ~$2.3M in total sales with a ~12% profit margin, averaging $28.66 profit per unit.
2. **The "Hero" Product**: Profitability is highly concentrated. The *Canon imageCLASS* printer alone generates over $25K in profit, highlighting a reliance on a few star products.
3. **Revenue vs. Profit Disconnect**: While sales show massive spikes (particularly in late 2017), profit remains relatively flat. This suggests that heavy discounting or rising operational costs are eating into margins during peak sales periods.
4. **Geographic Dominance**: The West region holds the largest customer base (32%), with California and New York City acting as the primary revenue hubs.
5. **Operational Efficiency**: Standard Class shipping accounts for nearly 60% of all orders, indicating that customers are highly sensitive to shipping costs, making expedited shipping a potential margin-killer.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Tool**: Microsoft Power BI Desktop
- **Language**: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom measures (e.g., Profit Margin %)
- **Design Principles**:conditional formatting for KPIs, and insight-driven chart titles.
